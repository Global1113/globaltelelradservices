Teleradiology Reporting Service

Global Tele Radiology Services is a leading provider of teleradiology solutions, committed to delivering accurate and timely online CT scan reports across both rural and urban regions worldwide. Our mission is to ensure uninterrupted access to expert radiology interpretation, enabling superior patient care globally. Our comprehensive services include 24×7×365 online reporting for:
X-rays
Contrast studies
CT and MRI scans
Mammography
3D reconstructions

Whether it's preliminary or final reports, we prioritize speed and accuracy to support both routine diagnostics and emergency healthcare needs. With a focus on reliability, precision, and rapid turnaround, we continuously strive to exceed the expectations of patients and healthcare institutions. Our commitment and consistency have earned us the reputation of being one of the most trusted teleradiology reporting companies in India.

Key Features of Our Teleradiology Services

Rapid Turnaround Time

At Global Tele Radiology Services, we understand that time is critical in patient care. We ensure high-quality radiology reporting with:
A standard turnaround time of 60 minutes
Emergency scans delivered within 30 minutes

Hassle-Free Technical Platform

Our services are powered by a state-of-the-art PACS system, offering:
Seamless and secure data handling
Full backend technical support
Automated monitoring of turnaround times through software and alert systems

Experienced Radiology Team

We maintain the highest standards in reporting by:
Conducting rigorous credential verification before onboarding teleradiologists
Performing regular clinical audits and review meetings
Implementing a system for tracking and managing reporting discrepancies

Flexible and Adaptive Services

We tailor our services to meet your operational needs. With integrated PACS and dedicated technical support, we offer a flexible solution that adapts to your workflow — ensuring smooth coordination between your team and ours.

Advanced 3D Reconstruction

We provide high-resolution 3D reconstruction images and videos to visualize:
Bony structures
Tumor positioning
Vascular pathways
Angiographic studies
These visuals aid physicians in making precise, informed treatment decisions.
