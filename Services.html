Precise interpretation and reporting

Remote radiology reporting, also known as teleradiology, involves the interpretation of diagnostic images like X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and PET CT scans by experienced radiologists who are not physically present at the diagnostic center where the images are captured. Accompanied by the patient's clinical history, these images are digitally transferred to a remote radiologist for accurate interpretation and reporting within a matter of hours. At Global Tele Radiology Services, we recognize the significance of practical experience in medicine and acknowledge the invaluable role of second opinions. Our network offers clients the option to seek real-time second opinions from our team of radiology experts, enhancing their understanding of diagnoses, enabling comprehensive treatment plans, and fostering improved prognoses. We understand that the true value of diagnosis lies in its accuracy and timely delivery. As one of India's premier teleradiology service providers, Global Tele Radiology Services is committed to delivering the most precise results within just a few hours. Our prompt interpretation and diagnosis ensure that patients receive life-saving interventions when needed — consistently. To support this mission, we've developed specialized AI-assisted algorithms across various specialties. These tools streamline the generation of comprehensive reports based on AI insights, optimizing workflow efficiency and productivity by identifying and flagging anomalies such as bleeds or critical underlying clinical issues. The synergy of our radiologists’ expertise and AI algorithms significantly reduces the “door-to-needle” time for hospitals and diagnostic centers. Global Tele Radiology Services envisions spearheading the transformation of India’s healthcare ecosystem into a more digital, organized, and resilient infrastructure. Our goal is to establish a pervasive diagnostics layer across the nation. With our dedicated radiology platform and advanced built-in AI systems, we are making measurable progress toward realizing this vision.
